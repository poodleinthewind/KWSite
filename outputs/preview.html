About

Info

Everything, for the love of the process.

It started with music. Then came huge paintings, writing on huge paintings, building things, working in advertising, tech, and design. I've been a high-rise window cleaner, a farmer, a painter, and a boogie woogie pianist. Motown and Southeastern Louisiana brass have been the most influential institutions of knowledge in my life.

Fifteen years of industrial tinkering — across agencies, startups, and in-house teams — has led me to Los Angeles, where I'm now focused on developing hybrid workflows that help us retain our individuality while executing at the scale and speed of AI.

Most often you'll find me in multifunctional roles where storytelling intersects with strategy and craft — translating complex technologies into clear, inspiring visual narrative. I don't over-index on tools, but I learn complex tech when it's the only way to communicate a dense idea.

More than ever, I feel an urgency and responsibility to carry intangibles like process, personality, and soul into everything I make. Rhythm is the god of creativity.
