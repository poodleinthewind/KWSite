Generative AI 3D After Effects Graphic Design

CoreWeave x Aston Martin F1

An AI brand film for CoreWeave and the Aston Martin Aramco F1 team. The brief was to make cloud infrastructure feel alive — to translate raw processing power into something visceral and cinematic.

Art direction, 3D, and animation across Cinema 4D, Octane, Redshift, MoonValley Marey, Cavalry, ComfyUI, and After Effects. Generative AI pipelines feeding 3D environments, hand-finished frame by frame.

Process

Mirror

Reflective CG environments built to visualize CoreWeave's processing power. The mirror sequences used Octane and Redshift to create infinite-depth compositions — light bouncing through layers of data.

Mirror — Process

Breaking down the mirror comp. Layers of reflection, refraction, and volumetric light stacked and timed to the edit. The raw process clips show how each element was isolated before final compositing in After Effects.

Wind Tunnel

Smoke simulations and wind tunnel visualizations tying the F1 aerodynamics story to CoreWeave's compute. Fluid dynamics rendered at high resolution, capturing the speed and precision of both worlds.

Wind Tunnel — Speed Tests

Iterating on smoke density, particle speed, and camera movement. These tests determined how fast the simulations could run before the aerodynamic detail broke down.

CRT & Signal

Analog textures on digital infrastructure. CRT scan lines and signal noise layered over data visualizations to ground the tech in something tactile and physical.

CRT — Extended

Longer CRT sequences exploring different degradation patterns — the visual language of data moving through hardware. Each pass was a different combination of interference and resolution.

Miniatures

Tilt-shift 3D renders of the Aston Martin at miniature scale — a visual device to make the massive feel intimate. Each angle composed to feel like a diorama you could hold in your hand.

Miniature — Depth

Depth pass renders used to control focus and atmospheric haze in the miniature sequences. The depth maps let us dial the tilt-shift effect precisely in post.

Tokyo Prism

A prismatic light study set in an urban environment. Refracted beams of data rendered through glass and metal — testing how CoreWeave's visual identity could live in the physical world.

Boxes

Geometric primitives choreographed to represent data architecture. Simple forms stacking, shifting, and reorganizing — the visual metaphor for compute infrastructure at work.

Smoke & X-Ray

Volumetric smoke passes combined with boolean geometry renders. The x-ray look stripped the car back to its structural bones — pure engineering visible through computational fog.

Desert

An environmental comp placing the F1 car in a desert landscape — testing how the visual system translated outside the track. Heat shimmer, dust, and light.

Moon

Lunar surface comp — pushing the visual language to its extreme. If CoreWeave's compute can take you anywhere, why not the moon. An exploration that tested the boundaries of the brand world.
