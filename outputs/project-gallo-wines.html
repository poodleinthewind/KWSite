Gallo Wines — Experiential

An immersive brand environment designed to bring Gallo's winemaking heritage to life at scale. The space needed to feel like an invitation into the brand's world — not a sales floor, not a museum, but something in between.

Tactile design, ambient storytelling, and interactive moments throughout created a continuous experience rather than a series of stations.

Process

Environment as argument

The space made a case for craft and heritage without making visitors sit through a presentation. Every design decision was an argument for the brand.

Flow and discovery

We mapped visitor movement patterns before finalizing the layout. The best moments in the space were the ones people stumbled into.
