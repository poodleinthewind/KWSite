About

Info

Everything, for the love of the process.

Most Kyle's on the internet started their careers first by pounding enormous amounts of monster energy drink, then gradually progressing to abstract expressionism via punching holes in drywall.

For me it started with music, from there it was making huge paintings, then writing on huge paintings, working on houses, working in advertising, working in tech, being ravenous in my pursuit to learn more about motion, 3D and of course design.

15 years of industrial tinkering has led me to Los Angeles, where I am currently focused on developing hybrid workflows that allow us to retain our individuality while executing at the scale and speed of AI.

As a visual designer it makes perfect sense that my core interests are in what we cannot see. The beginnings of stories unfolding between a person, an animal, a place, a motivation, a brand. . . .

More than ever I feel an urgency and responsibility to bring these intangibles like process, personality, and soul to everything that I make.
