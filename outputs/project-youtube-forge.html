Event Design Brand Identity

Forge

A special event concept created for YouTube to help the platform stop time for a moment to think deeply about the best way to step into the future. YouTube functions as both a video platform and a portal to a bottomless galaxy of content where time ceases to exist.

Client
YouTube
Role
Creative Director, Lead Designer, Animator
Agency
Jack Morton
Process

Depth as Identity

The foundational idea involved sending a simple structure — a square — back into space to see what a cascade of layers could communicate. Brand authenticity surfaced in the voids in-between more prominently than in the actual depth itself.

Threading the Look

Threading the look across multiple formats — from branding to physical stage installation to digital animation — created a cohesive brand experience. Deliverables included visual identity lockup, collateral design, stage design, on-screen content, animation direction, and merchandise.
