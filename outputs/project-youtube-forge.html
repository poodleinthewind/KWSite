Forge

Forge — YouTube

Sometimes it's hard to tell whether YouTube is just a video platform or a portal to a bottomless galaxy of content where time ceases to exist. Naturally with all that depth comes responsibility, and that's why YouTube invited us to create a vision for Forge — a special event where we could help them stop time for a moment to think deeply about the best way to step into the future.

Client
YouTube
Role

Agency

Process

The Voids In-Between

The infinite expanse of content on YouTube can't exist without structure. A simple structure — a square — was sent back into space to see what a cascade of layers could communicate. As the idea evolved, more brand authenticity surfaced in the voids in-between than in the actual depth itself.

Threading the Look

An impactful lockup, collateral, stage design, on-screen content, and animation direction were just a few of the items that extended the look.

IRL

It all came alive in a very real way on the stage that the amazing Matt Gates interpreted from crude pencil sketches and Photoshop comps.
