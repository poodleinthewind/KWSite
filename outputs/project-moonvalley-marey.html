Generative AI Film Motion

Marey

MoonValley invited me to help produce the release video for Marey — their first studio-grade, frame-perfect AI video tool, trained exclusively on licensed data. The goal was to express a new sense of control in a chaotic creative landscape.

We needed to communicate the importance of creative intention. This era is no longer about how you got there, but what was the idea. Can you visualize the intent? Choreograph the motion curves in your mind? Then this is the product for you. Working alongside the otherworldly creatives at Asteria Film allowed us to explode processes and push ideas far outside of the boundaries into entirely new conceptual terrain.
