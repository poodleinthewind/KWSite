Generative AI Film Motion

Marey

MoonValley invited me to help produce the release video for Marey — their first studio-grade, frame-perfect AI video tool, trained exclusively on licensed data. The goal was to express a new sense of control in a chaotic creative landscape. The result: an epochal journey shaped by a fluid, nonlinear approach to filmmaking.

001. Training the Models

Control in a chaotic landscape.

Marey is the first AI video tool trained exclusively on licensed data — a new standard. The creative challenge was making that precision feel visceral and cinematic rather than mechanical. We built toward a fluid, nonlinear narrative: not a demo, but an experience.

002. Setting the Look

Tools as a creative act.

To guide the models, we created a series of base visuals using C4D, After Effects, and a suite of custom, vibe-coded parametric tools built in Claude.ai — including a tunnel generator, geometric wave field visualizer, sine wave system, and more.
